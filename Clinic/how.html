 Get Started

 

Do you have an idea for a crowdsourcing challenge? Are you in the process of organizing your challenge? Have you already hosted a crowdsourcing challenge?

 

Step 1

Register your idea/contest using the form below, and receive a custom starter pack for your contest.

Step 2

Our expert advising team will get back to you shortly with the best resources and tools to take your contest to the next phase.

Step 3

Empower other contest organizers by joining our group to share the impact of crowdsourcing.

 

Register your challenge for FREE below to gain access to some of our wonderful resources:

  1. Expert advice and access to our Global Innovation Hubs

  2. Documents from our past challenges, including

    1. How to convene a steering committee

    2. Calls for submission

    3. Promotional materials and Infographics

    4. FAQ’s

    5. Judging Rubrics

    6. Choosing a prize structure

    7. Protocols for sharing contest results

  3. Download your very own starter pack that includes easy to use templates to create your very own challenge!

  4. Partnership and in-person planning with the SESH team!

 
 

Register your challenge today.
